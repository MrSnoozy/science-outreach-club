Synchrotron Standard Reference Libraries

To accurately run Linear Combination Fitting (LCF) sweeps on our data, our team references certified baseline mineral profiles. These open-access structural data logs are used to verify our spectral alignments:

  • Iron Reference Standards: High-purity spectra vectors for Magnetite (\(\text{Fe}_3\text{O}_4\)), Hematite (\(\alpha\text{-Fe}_2\text{O}_3\)), Maghemite, and Ferrihydrite.
  • Manganese Reference Standards: High-purity absorption baselines for Manganosite (\(\text{MnO}\)), Pyrochroite, and various complex multi-valence organic models.
