About the Club

Science Outreach Club is a student-led organization that nurtures STEM curiosity through hands-on projects, mentorship, and competitions.

The Science Outreach Club welcomes students of all backgrounds to collaborate on engineering and scientific projects. We run subteams focused on CanSat (building sattellites that will be launched and record data), Student on the Beamline (SotB), and attending an annual Science Fair to showcase student research. Meetings are weekly; all skill levels are welcome.

Meetings: Thursdays at lunch - Mr. Manning's room - Room 103
