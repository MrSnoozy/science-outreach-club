Science Fair

Annual showcase for student research and creative projects.

About the Science Fair

The Science Outreach club will have a focus on participating in the South Fraser Regional Science Fair. It is a regional event where students present their STEM related research, experiments, or innovative projects. It gives an opportunity for students your age to explore topics they are passionate about or to even explore new topics. From this exploration you present your idea to judges and win new opportunities and awards! These competitions range from regional, to national, and even international showcases, so why not explore, create, and innovate a new project while you have the time!
